### **2. Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)**
**Add to:** Lower panel (oscillator)  
**Settings:** 21 period  
**Why:** Shows accumulation (buying pressure) vs distribution (selling pressure). Volume confirmation.  
**How:** CMF > 0 = bullish, CMF < 0 = bearish. Divergence = reversal signal.

---

### **3. Volume Profile** (Fixed Range)
**Add to:** Main price chart (right side histogram)  
**Settings:** Session-based or fixed range  
**Why:** Shows WHERE volume traded. Better than S/R lines.  
**How:** POC (Point of Control) = strongest S/R. Low Volume Nodes = gaps to fill.

---

### **4. Bollinger Bands**
**Add to:** Main price chart (overlay)  
**Settings:** 20 period, 2 standard deviations  
**Why:** Volatility + mean reversion. Visual confirmation of your Squeeze Momentum.  
**How:** Tight bands = squeeze (breakout coming). Price at upper/lower band = overbought/oversold.

---

### **5. DXY Correlation Overlay**
**Add to:** Split screen or indicator overlay  
**Settings:** Invert DXY scale  
**Why:** Crypto moves INVERSE to Dollar. Strong dollar = weak BTC.  
**How:** DXY falling = BTC bullish. DXY rising = BTC bearish.
